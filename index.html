    Commonly asked questions

    Who can enroll themselves in CUET Online programe?

    Any student who is going to apply for CUET exam in 2025 and wants to start an early preparation, can enroll for this course.

    What physical materials will the student get for the Board examination?

    Workbook comprising Multiple Choice Questions, notes and class exercises for Practice.

    What will be the time duration of each class?

    Each class will be of 90 mins.

    Since General Knowledge and current affairs are a part of General test, how will the program help in the preparation of that?

    A student will get classes, GK updates and Compendium through the GKZone tab present on Myzone portal.

    Will there be a parent-teacher's meeting/s?

    Yes. During the course, there will be parent-teacher's meeting.

    How many mocks will be there?

    There will be 40 mocks for each subject.

    In case, a live class is missed. Will we get a recording of the session?

    Yes, Recordings are available for all the live sessions.

    How many classes will be there in a week?

    There will be 1-2 classes for each subject in a week.

    If I am unable to take the mock Test on the same day, is it possible to take it later?

    Yes. Once a mock is live, a student can take it any day.

    Would the faculty have their cameras on while teaching?

    Yes, the faculties as well as the students will keep their cameras on while teaching.

    Will the program help in the preparation of ICSE/State Boards?

    The Board preparation will be according to the CBSE Board. However, ICSE/State Board students can join the program for their CUET preparation.

    Will there be mock examinations for Board exams?

    No. In case you also look forward to prepare for CBSE Class XII Board exam, you must join our CUET + Boards 2025 prep program.

    Will there be tests after a chapter is completed?

    Yes. There will be 2-chapter tests consisting of 10 questions each.

    Can parents access the Myzone portal of their child?

    Parents will get the password of the Myzone portal of their child initially, so parents can check the portal if the child does not change the password.

    Are mock Tests based on the pattern of CUET exam?

    Yes, the mocks will be based on the actual CUET pattern.

    Can students ask doubts during the live classes?

    Yes, a student can ask their doubts in the live class.
